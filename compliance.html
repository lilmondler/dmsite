Audit trail

Every change to study data is written to an immutable log, designed so the full history of a record can be reconstructed for inspection — a principle the platform shares with how blockchain ledgers prevent retroactive edits.

Access control

Role-based permissions govern who can view, enter or change data at each stage of a study, with access logged alongside the audit trail.

Data protection

Data is hosted in a private-cloud (SaaS) environment on attested infrastructure, designed to meet the requirements of 152-FZ alongside HIPAA and GDPR.

About DM365

Built by a team that has worked inside clinical data, not just around it

Data Management 365 builds MainEDC, an EDC and IRT platform used across Phase I–IV studies by sponsors, CROs and research teams in Russia and internationally.

Who we are

DM365 is based in Saint Petersburg, Russia, and has built and supported MainEDC across studies covering more than 215,000 patients and 3,100+ research sites in 20 countries, including trials behind FDA-approved drugs.

The company focuses specifically on clinical data management — not as one product line among many, but as the core of what it builds.

What we build

MainEDC combines EDC, IWRS/randomization, eConsent, ePRO/eCOA, AI-assisted coding and document management into one validated platform, so study teams aren't stitching together separate point solutions.
